PURPOSE:
The Blue Ice Highway System connects the Oceanic Spawn Platform to all
four cardinal realms. Players can travel at incredible speeds using
boats on blue ice, making the spawn platform the transportation hub
of the entire Aethelgard Nexus.

HIGHWAY DESIGN:
The realm highways are separate projects (one per realm), but they
connect to the spawn platform at these transition points:

- NORTH HIGHWAY: Starts at Z:-40, continues to Northern Realm
- SOUTH HIGHWAY: Starts at Z:+40, continues to Southern Realm
- EAST HIGHWAY: Starts at X:+40, continues to Eastern Realm
- WEST HIGHWAY: Starts at X:-40, continues to Western Realm

================================================================================
                         BOAT LAUNCHER SYSTEM
================================================================================

For OUTBOUND travel (from spawn to realm), players simply:
1. Place boat on blue ice at platform entrance
2. Enter boat
3. Hold W (forward) key
4. Boat accelerates on ice
5. Exit pier at high speed toward realm

For INBOUND travel (from realm to spawn), an OPTIONAL launcher
helps slow down and stop at the dock:

LAUNCHER DESIGN (at dock end):

Water brake system:
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ When boat arrives at high speed:                                │
│ 1. Hits water section at dock (Z:-31 to -32)                   │
│ 2. Water friction slows boat dramatically                       │
│ 3. Boat stops in dock area                                      │
│ 4. Player exits safely                                          │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

ALTERNATIVE: Soul sand slowdown
Place soul sand under water at dock to further slow boats.

================================================================================
                         MAINTENANCE NOTES
================================================================================

BLUE ICE PROPERTIES:
├── Does NOT melt (unlike regular ice)
├── Slipperiest ice type in game
├── Obtained from: Ice Spikes biome, igloos, or crafting
├── Crafting: 9 Packed Ice → 1 Blue Ice
├── Packed Ice: 9 Regular Ice → 1 Packed Ice
└── NEVER place torches near ice (packed ice melts)

RESTOCKING DOCKS:
├── Check boat chests regularly
├── Players may take boats and not return them
├── Keep spare boats in storage
└── Consider hopper system for auto-restocking (advanced)

REPAIRS:
├── Glass roof may break (creeper explosions, accidents)
├── Keep spare glass in dock chest
├── Walls rarely need repair
└── Ice doesn't degrade naturally
