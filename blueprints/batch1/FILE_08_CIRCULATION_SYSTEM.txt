PURPOSE:
This document details ALL methods for moving between zones and areas
of the Oceanic Spawn Platform. A well-designed circulation system
ensures players can navigate efficiently and safely.

================================================================================
                         CIRCULATION OVERVIEW
================================================================================

VERTICAL MOVEMENT (between zones):
├── Bubble Columns (Soul Sand) ........ Rapid ascent
├── Bubble Columns (Magma) ............ Controlled descent
├── Spiral Staircases ................. Inside towers
├── Ladders ........................... Backup access everywhere
└── Water Elevator Shaft .............. Central column option

HORIZONTAL MOVEMENT (within zones):
├── Walking ........................... Main platform, piers
├── Swimming .......................... Underwater chamber
├── Blue Ice Boats .................... Piers to realms
└── Bridge ............................ Between towers at Y:75

CONSTRUCTION STEPS FOR EACH SHAFT:

1. PREPARE THE SHAFT
   ├── Clear 2×1 area from Y:40 to Y:64
   ├── Build glass or solid walls around shaft
   └── Ensure shaft is completely enclosed

2. PLACE BOTTOM BLOCKS (Y:41)
   ├── Soul sand on one side (creates upward bubbles)
   └── Magma block on adjacent side (creates downward bubbles)

3. FILL WITH WATER
   ├── Place water source at TOP (Y:63)
   ├── Water must be SOURCE blocks, not flowing
   └── Use kelp trick: plant kelp, let it grow, then remove
       (converts flowing water to source blocks)

4. VERIFY BUBBLES
   ├── Soul sand should show upward bubble particles
   ├── Magma should show downward bubble particles
   └── Test by entering - should shoot up or sink down

5. CREATE EXIT AT TOP (Y:64)
   ├── Trapdoor at Y:64 level (water-logged)
   ├── Sign on wall to hold water back
   └── Player emerges onto platform safely

================================================================================
                    UNDERWATER CHAMBER ACCESS
================================================================================

The underwater chamber (Y:42-55) can be accessed via:

1. BUBBLE COLUMNS (primary method)
   - Descend via magma column
   - Enter chamber through floor opening
   - Ascend via soul sand column

2. ANCHOR PILLAR STAIRCASES (backup method)
   - Each corner pillar has internal staircase
   - From Y:40 to Y:64
   - Narrow but functional

ANCHOR PILLAR STAIRCASE DETAIL:

Pillar dimensions: 7×7
Staircase is inside the pillar, spiraling upward.

================================================================================
                         LADDER BACKUP SYSTEM
================================================================================

Ladders provide emergency access if other systems fail.

LADDER POSITIONS:

1. INSIDE BUBBLE COLUMN SHAFTS
   - Ladders on one wall of each shaft
   - From Y:40 to Y:64
   - For climbing if water drains

2. INSIDE ANCHOR PILLARS
   - Backup to internal stairs
   - From Y:40 to Y:64
   - On back wall of each pillar

3. OUTSIDE BEACON TOWERS
   - Back side of each tower (away from spawn)
   - From Y:64 to Y:84
   - For quick emergency ascent

4. PIER EDGES
   - Side of each pier
   - From water level to pier top
   - For players who fall in water

LADDER PLACEMENT RULES:
├── Always place on SOLID blocks (not glass)
├── Ensure continuous path (no gaps)
├── Place lantern at top and bottom for visibility
└── Test climb before finalizing

         Y:90 ┌───┐  Tower level
              │ . │

              │ W │

              │ W │

OPERATING PRINCIPLE:
- Single shaft, player chooses direction
- Soul sand at bottom = always goes UP
- To descend: player must swim down against current
- OR: Install pistons to swap soul sand/magma

RECOMMENDATION: Use the four corner shafts instead of central elevator.
The corner shafts don't disrupt the spawn point design and provide
redundancy (4 access points instead of 1).

================================================================================
                    BRIDGE CIRCULATION (Y:75)
================================================================================

The bridge connecting the two beacon towers at Y:75 allows
horizontal movement between towers without descending.

BRIDGE SPECIFICATIONS:
├── Length: 14 blocks (Z:-7 to Z:+7)
├── Width: 3 blocks (X:-1 to X:+1)
├── Height: Y:75 (floor) to Y:76 (railings)
└── Connects: North Tower ↔ South Tower

ACCESS TO BRIDGE:
1. From North Tower: Exit at Y:75 observation deck, walk south
2. From South Tower: Exit at Y:75 observation deck, walk north
3. From Platform: Climb either tower to Y:75

BRIDGE FEATURES:
├── Safety railings on both sides
├── 3 lanterns for lighting
├── Clear glass sections for views (optional)
└── Central meeting point at X:0, Z:0

VIEW FROM BRIDGE:
Standing at center of bridge (Y:75), you can see:
- Below: The entire surface platform and spawn point
- North: North beacon tower and cyan beam
- South: South beacon tower and magenta beam
- East/West: Ocean extending to horizon
- Up: Both beacon beams crossing the sky

================================================================================
                    PIER CIRCULATION
================================================================================

Each pier provides passage from the platform to the realm highways.

PIER ENTRY (from platform):
├── 5-block wide opening in platform wall
├── Smooth transition from stone to ice
└── Clear signage indicating realm direction

PIER TRAVEL:
├── Blue ice path (3 blocks wide)
├── Walls prevent falling off sides
├── Glass roof prevents falling from above
└── Average travel speed: Very fast (boats on ice)

PIER EXIT (to realm highway):
├── Dock at end of pier (Z:-30, +30 or X:-30, +30)
├── 2 chests with supplies (boats, food)
├── Water section for boat launch
└── Connection point to realm highway

PIER BOAT STORAGE:

Each dock has:
├── Chest 1: Oak Boats (×32)
├── Chest 2: Food supplies (bread, cooked porkchop)
├── Sign: "To [Realm Name]"
└── Optional: Map of realm in item frame

================================================================================
                    ENTRY FROM WATER
================================================================================

Players arriving by boat or swimming can enter the platform via:

1. PIER DOCKS (recommended)
   - Swim to any pier end
   - Climb slab steps out of water
   - Walk up pier to platform

2. PLATFORM EDGE SLABS
   - Slabs around platform perimeter at Y:63.5
   - Allow climbing out of water
   - Located every 5 blocks around edge

3. BUBBLE COLUMN ENTRY (underwater)
   - Dive down to any shaft entrance
   - Enter soul sand column
   - Ride bubbles up to platform

WATER ENTRY DETAIL:

Side view of platform edge:

Y:63.5│  S  │      Slab (step from water)

      └─────┘

S = Bottom slab (allows climbing out)
w = Wall (polished blackstone wall)
k = Floor (polished blackstone)

================================================================================
                    EMERGENCY EXITS
================================================================================

In case of emergency (mobs, flooding, etc.), players should know:

1. FASTEST ASCENT: Bubble column (soul sand) - instant
2. FASTEST DESCENT: Magma column - instant
3. ESCAPE TO OCEAN: Jump off platform edge (Y:64 → water)
4. ESCAPE TO TOWER: Enter tower, close iron door

SAFE ZONES:
├── Surface platform: Fully lit, no mob spawns
├── Underwater chamber: Lit, conduit protection
├── Tower interiors: Enclosed, doors closeable
└── Bridge: Open but lit

================================================================================
                    CONSTRUCTION PRIORITY
================================================================================

Build circulation elements in this order:

1. BUBBLE COLUMNS (essential for construction)
   - Build first - you need them to move materials
   - Start with one shaft, add others as needed

2. PLATFORM EDGE ACCESS
   - Add slabs around edge
   - Allows water entry during construction

3. TOWER STAIRCASES
   - Build as towers go up
   - Add landings at observation decks

4. LADDERS
   - Add last as backup system
   - Place after main circulation complete

5. SIGNAGE
   - Add after all paths finalized
   - Verify accuracy before permanent placement
