================================================================================
                    THE OCEANIC SPAWN PLATFORM - FILE 12
                       UNDERWATER CHAMBER DETAIL
                           The Aethelgard Nexus
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
The Underwater Chamber is the mystical heart of the foundation - a
15×15 glass-walled room where players can observe marine life, access
the conduit's power, and discover hidden secrets of the deep.

================================================================================
                         CHAMBER SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

DIMENSIONS:
├── Interior: 15×15 blocks (225 square blocks floor area)
├── Exterior: 17×17 blocks (including walls)
├── Height: 9 blocks (Y:42 to Y:50)
├── Location: Center of structure (X:-7 to +7, Z:-7 to +7)
└── Entry Points: 4 bubble columns at corners

ELEVATION:
├── Floor: Y:42 (dark prismarine)
├── Walls: Y:42-50 (glass)
├── Ceiling: Y:50 (prismarine with glass panels)
├── Conduit: Y:48 (center pedestal)
└── Access: Bubble columns from Y:40 to Y:64

================================================================================
                         ACCESS POINTS
================================================================================

BUBBLE COLUMN ENTRY/EXIT:
Located in the four anchor pillars (outside chamber walls)

Entry procedure:
1. Descend via magma block column (outside pillar)
2. Exit column at Y:43 through door
3. Enter chamber through pillar doorway
4. Reverse to exit via soul sand column

PILLAR DOORWAYS (from chamber to bubble shafts):

NW Pillar Access: X:-8, Z:-8 (door at Y:43)
NE Pillar Access: X:+8, Z:-8 (door at Y:43)
SW Pillar Access: X:-8, Z:+8 (door at Y:43)
SE Pillar Access: X:+8, Z:+8 (door at Y:43)

INTERNAL STAIRCASE (optional):
If you want stairs instead of bubble columns:
├── Location: Inside one anchor pillar
├── Spiral design from Y:40 to Y:63
├── Slower but more immersive
└── See FILE 08 for staircase details
