================================================================================
                    PHASE 1: OCEAN FLOOR PREPARATION
                         (Y:40-42) - Est. 1-2 hours
================================================================================

OBJECTIVE: Prepare a stable foundation on the ocean floor.

STEP 1.1: Locate Build Site
├── Navigate to X:0, Z:0 (world origin)
├── Ensure location is in deep ocean biome
├── Verify ocean floor is approximately Y:40
└── Mark the exact center with a temporary block

STEP 1.2: Clear Ocean Floor
├── Area to clear: 35×35 blocks centered on X:0, Z:0
├── Remove any kelp, seagrass, coral
├── Level uneven terrain (fill holes, remove bumps)
├── Use sponges if needed to create work area
└── Floor should be flat at Y:40

STEP 1.3: Mark Corner Positions
Place temporary markers (wool or concrete) at anchor pillar corners:
├── NW Pillar: X:-12, Z:-12
├── NE Pillar: X:+12, Z:-12
├── SW Pillar: X:-12, Z:+12
└── SE Pillar: X:+12, Z:+12

STEP 1.4: Establish Work Area
├── Place torches/sea lanterns for visibility
├── Set up temporary bubble column for access
├── Store building materials in nearby chests
└── Consider building temporary underwater shelter

MATERIALS NEEDED (Phase 1):
├── Sponges: 20-50 (for water removal if needed)
├── Temporary markers: 20 blocks
├── Torches/Sea lanterns: 30
└── Chests: 4-6 (material storage)

================================================================================
                    PHASE 2: ANCHOR PILLARS
                         (Y:40-55) - Est. 3-4 hours
================================================================================

OBJECTIVE: Build four massive 7×7 pillars from ocean floor to spire level.

STEP 2.1: Build NW Pillar First
Location: X:-15 to -9, Z:-15 to -9

Layer by layer from Y:40 upward:
├── Y:40: Full 7×7 prismarine base
├── Y:41-42: Continue 7×7, leave center hollow (5×5 interior)
├── Y:43-54: Continue with interior (future staircase space)
├── Y:55: Cap layer connecting to spire
└── Mark bubble column positions at X:-12, Z:-12

STEP 2.2: Build Remaining Pillars
Repeat Step 2.1 for:
├── NE Pillar: X:+9 to +15, Z:-15 to -9
├── SW Pillar: X:-15 to -9, Z:+9 to +15
└── SE Pillar: X:+9 to +15, Z:+9 to +15

STEP 2.3: Install Temporary Access
├── Place soul sand at Y:41 for one pillar (rapid ascent)
├── Fill water column with water sources (kelp method)
├── Test ascent to surface
└── This becomes primary construction access

STEP 2.4: Verify Alignment
├── Stand at center (X:0, Z:0)
├── Confirm all four pillars are symmetrical
├── Check pillar tops are level at Y:55
└── Adjust if necessary before continuing

MATERIALS NEEDED (Phase 2):
├── Prismarine: ~800 blocks
├── Dark Prismarine: ~400 blocks
├── Sea Lanterns: 48 (12 per pillar)
├── Soul Sand: 4 blocks
└── Kelp: 1 stack (for water source conversion)

================================================================================
                    PHASE 3: UNDERWATER CHAMBER
                         (Y:42-50) - Est. 2-3 hours
================================================================================

OBJECTIVE: Create the 15×15 glass-walled viewing chamber.

STEP 3.1: Build Chamber Floor (Y:42)
├── Clear 17×17 area between pillars
├── Lay dark prismarine floor from X:-7 to +7, Z:-7 to +7
├── Add prismarine border
├── Install floor sea lanterns at marked positions
└── Build conduit pedestal base at center

STEP 3.2: Build Chamber Walls (Y:43-49)
├── Place corner prismarine pillars (X:±7, Z:±7)
├── Fill between pillars with glass
├── Work from bottom up, one layer at a time
├── Ensure complete seal (no gaps!)
└── Use sponges to remove water as you build

STEP 3.3: Build Chamber Ceiling (Y:50)
├── Prismarine frame around edges
├── Glass panels in center (skylights)
├── Sea lanterns at designated positions
├── Leave 3×3 opening above conduit position
└── Seal completely

STEP 3.4: Remove Water from Chamber
├── Enter chamber via temporary opening
├── Place sponges to absorb water (wet sponges)
├── Dry sponges in furnace, repeat
├── Continue until chamber is completely dry
└── Verify all walls are sealed

STEP 3.5: Add Chamber Furniture
├── Place conduit pedestal (but NOT conduit yet)
├── Add ender chest, respawn anchor, crafting table
├── Place bookshelves and armor stands
├── Install item frames
└── Add decorative coral

MATERIALS NEEDED (Phase 3):
├── Dark Prismarine: 196 blocks
├── Prismarine: 124 blocks
├── Prismarine Bricks: 45 blocks
├── Glass: 485 blocks (walls + ceiling)
├── Sea Lanterns: 18 blocks

└── Sponges: 30-50 (for water removal)
